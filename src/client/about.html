About Express Blog

Welcome to Express Blog, a full-featured blogging platform built with Node.js, Express, and PostgreSQL.

Our mission is to provide a clean, fast, and secure space for writers and readers to share and discover ideas. Users can register, create their own posts, and engage with a community of creators.
